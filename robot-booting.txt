To connect to the robot:
   connect to the ROBOTIS-OP3-share hidden network
   ssh robotis@10.42.0.1
       111111

To turn on the demo stuff:
   roslaunch op3_demo demo.launch
   On webbrowser, go to 10.42.0.1

If you kill the roslaunch command, reload the webpage after restarting it and it will 
be able to start doing things again
